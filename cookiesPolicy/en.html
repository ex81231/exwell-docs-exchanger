Cookie Policy of Exwell Service

Exwell uses cookies to provide an optimal user experience when interacting with our platform. These files help personalize the service, analyze the effectiveness of the platform, and continuously improve our services. To fully access all Exwell functions, it is necessary to activate cookies on all devices (computers, smartphones, tablets, and other gadgets).

Important: By using the Exwell service with browser settings that allow the acceptance of cookies, you automatically consent to their use. If you do not want to provide such permission, you can deactivate cookies in your browser settings; however, this may negatively affect the functionality of the service and the quality of interaction with the platform..

What are Cookies?

Cookies are small pieces of data that are sent from the Exwell website and stored on your device in the browser for further use.

Why does Exwell use Cookies?

We use cookies to collect information about how users interact with our platform. This data allows us to remember your device, browser, and preferences when using Exwell services. Monitoring and analyzing this information enables us to improve the functionality of the platform and adapt the user interface to your needs. The decision to enable or disable cookies is entirely within your competence; however, without them, the operation of our service may be significantly limited.

How to disable Cookies?

You can manage cookies at any time through your web browser settings, including the ability to completely delete them. Browser settings allow you to deactivate cookies; however, Exwell does not guarantee the full functionality of all services with such restrictions. If you accidentally disabled cookies, they can be easily activated in your browser's settings section.
